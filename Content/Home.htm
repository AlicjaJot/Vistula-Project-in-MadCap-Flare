About that guide

Your team is happy about working remotely, but you’ve had enough of it? Your coworkers would quit if they had to go back to the office and yet you miss face-to-face meetings? You hear everywhere how great home office is, but you only see it’s disadvantages?

Maybe you are happy about remote work, but you do not have the motivation when you work at home, it is getting more and more difficult to wake up for morning meetings, you do not get along with your team and your professional and private life are blending together? Decline in general well-being and other psychological symptoms might be connected to working in home office mode.

This guide will help you identify your problems with remote work, it will show you possible solutions and will be a great companion in the process of change and introducing healthy habits.

Remember that you do not have to change your work, to wrap your head around remote work. Expand your knowledge about operating in home office and enjoy all of its perks.
